“Pick and Share” is a social platform in the form of a mobile application that seeks to find food/items that might go to waste and link them to the person needing it.

Get started

What is Pick & Share?

Pick & Share unites neighbours with each other so that excess food/items can be shared rather than disposed. This app can also be used for extra household items for pleasure

Pick & Share is a breeze to use! To make an item available for pick-up, simply launch the app and add a photo, descriptions, and where and when the item is accessible.

To get stuff, simply look through the available posts near you, request what you want, and organize a pick-up via private chat.
